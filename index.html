About Me

Hello! I am a passionate data scientist with a strong foundation in Python, SQL, and Tableau, and a drive for transforming complex datasets into clear, actionable insights. My experience spans building predictive models, designing interactive dashboards, and developing end-to-end data pipelines that bridge raw data to impactful decisions.

I have worked on projects ranging from NCAA basketball analytics and baseball statistics to real-world applications of machine learning and data visualization. Whether it is exploring large databases with SQL, crafting insightful visual stories with Tableau, or prototyping machine learning models in Python, I thrive at the intersection of data and problem-solving.

When I am not experimenting with new data science techniques, I enjoy running, watching sports, and using my data analytical skills to excel in Fantasy Football.
